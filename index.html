Cotton beanies

Hand made in England using mercerised cotton yarn. Our head knitter Joan J has many years experience. We use only 100% cotton because it's cool. Most of the hats are made using DK (double knit) cotton yarn which is lighter than standard DK and is suitable for all year round use. A few hats have been knitted with a thicker DK yarn more suitable for winter wear. All the hats vary slightly in size and they gradually shape to the head after wearing and washing. The majority of the hats are suitable for small/medium adult. All sizes are approximate. All colours are subject to variations in display devices. These hats are suitable for patients with chemotherapy and alopecia.
